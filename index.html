Recycler App

Recycler App is a mobile web application that functions as a digital waste bank, allowing users to sell recyclable materials such as plastic, metal, and paper in exchange for monetary rewards.

The application features two main roles: users, who sell their waste, and admins, who manage waste collection and processing. Users can request a pickup, and an admin will confirm the request and schedule the collection.

The app provides a notification system with statuses such as "Pending Confirmation", "Pickup in Progress", and "Completed". Once the waste is collected and verified, users receive payments based on the type and weight of the materials submitted.

Recycler App aims to promote environmental awareness by making recycling more accessible and rewarding.

Tobacco Trade Information System

The Tobacco Merchant Information System is a web-based project I developed as my final university project to help merchants manage their inventory, sales, and transactions more efficiently. This system replaces the traditional method of using Excel spreadsheets with a user-friendly digital platform, improving data accuracy and accessibility.

Office Room Reservation

The Office Room Reservation Website is a system I designed to make it easier to book meeting rooms in an office environment. This project is part of a fictional company called Dunder Mifflin, which is inspired by the company featured in the popular TV series The Office. The idea behind this website is to provide employees or staff with a simple, user-friendly platform where they can check room availability, schedule a meeting, and confirm their reservation without the hassle of manual booking. For the development, I used Vue.js as the main framework to build the front-end of the application, ensuring a responsive and interactive user experience. To handle the data, such as storing reservation details, I utilized the browser’s local storage, which allows the website to save and retrieve booking information directly on the client side without requiring a dedicated backend server. You can try the live demo here: Conference Room Website

Shadow Boxing

Shadow Boxing is a popular game that went viral on the internet, where there are 2 players facing each other. Each player chooses a side and tries to outsmart their opponent by predicting their next move. The gameplay is simple yet exciting — one player throws a punch while the other tries to dodge at the right moment. Because of its fast reflexes, mind-reading aspect, and competitive nature, Shadow Boxing became widely shared online, often seen in short videos and challenges.
